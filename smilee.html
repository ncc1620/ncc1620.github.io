About SmiLee

SmiLee is the long‑standing creative persona of Lee Richard Grimwade, a Los Angeles–based musician, filmmaker, documentarian, and multidisciplinary artist active since the late 1990s. The name “SmiLee” appears across music, live performance, documentary film, photography, improvisation, and mythic storytelling, forming a unified creative identity that spans decades.

As a public figure, SmiLee is known for:

  • Live music performance and collaborative improvisation
  • Documentary filmmaking and concert films
  • Photography and visual storytelling
  • Mythic worldbuilding and symbolic narrative design
  • Community‑centered creative practice
  • Archival preservation and cultural documentation

This page serves as the official identity hub for the persona, consolidating its history, lineage, creative works, and public references.
