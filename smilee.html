About

SmiLee is the long‑standing multi-disciplinary creative persona of Lee Richard Grimwade — an improvisational, proximity‑based, community‑centered identity active since at least 1998. The persona spans documentary practice, live music, photography, mythic storytelling, and symbolic worldbuilding. SmiLee weaves together concert films, artist stories, symbolic frameworks, and community‑rooted creative practice into a living archive of culture and connection. SmiLee functions as the expressive performative, and mythic layer of the YOU ARE HERE creative ecosystem.

Music Lineage

SmiLee appears throughout the musical lineage of the YOU ARE HERE universe — from collaborative improvisation to solo performance, songwriting, and experimental sound work.
