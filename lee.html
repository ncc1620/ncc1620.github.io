About

British‑born interdisciplinary creative, documentarian, musician, and founder of the YOU ARE HERE creative ecosystem. Lee’s work spans documentary practice, worldbuilding, music, community‑centered storytelling, and the development of symbolic and mythic frameworks that inform multiple creative identities and projects — including the long‑standing creative persona, SmiLee.

Creative Houses

Lee is the founder and creative steward of the YOU ARE HERE ecosystem — a parent organization that anchors multiple creative houses. Blackbird Pie Media focuses on documentary and post‑production work, while Wanderful Wizard Productions develops mythic systems, symbolic frameworks, and worldbuilding structures.

Music Projects

Lee maintains a musical lineage through both collaborative and solo projects. YOU ARE HERE (Band) represents the early collaborative era that later informed the YOU ARE HERE creative philosophy. The Zephyr Project functions as a solo music identity focused on songwriting, recording, and experimental sound work.
